Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Overview

Carbon Snitch ("we", "us") builds free Android apps. This policy explains what data is collected when you use our apps and website.

Data We Collect

Our apps are free and ad-supported. We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Interaction data with ads

This data is collected and processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy for full details.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email, account details, or any personally identifiable information directly. We do not store gameplay data on our own servers.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

Children

Our apps are rated "Everyone" on Google Play. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
